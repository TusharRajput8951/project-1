Tushar Motorcycle Club Mission:

To promote the existence of motorcycling as a way of life. To embody the story of the Indian road. To live as sovereign beings, resisting that and those who would limit our freedoms, virtuous as their causes may be. To create, explore and thrive in the ever changing landscape of motion.
